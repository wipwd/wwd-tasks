Welcome to wip:wd's Tasks!

This project allows you to manage your tasks, Kanban style, directly in your browser. We do not keep, or have access, to any of your data.

All tasks, and their associated data, is kept in your browser's storage.

While this has the obvious benefit of YOU keeping control of your data, it has the drawback of this data not being shared across devices, or even across browsers. It will however survive browser sessions (e.g., when you shutdown your computer or close your tab).

Be aware that, at this point in time, this is still under heavy development. Therefore, we cannot guarantee your data will be safe from deletion, should a format change be needed, or a bug surfaces. We will do our very best to prevent that from happening.
